How It Works

Your Outgoing Mail — Managed From Start to Finish

We fit into your existing workflow and take the daily mail burden off your team completely.

1

We Assess Your Needs

We start by understanding your mail volume, outgoing frequency, and any special requirements — bulk mail, certified letters, metered postage — and build a pickup schedule around your operations.

2

Scheduled Daily Pickup

Our courier arrives at your office at a consistent time each day. We collect all outgoing mail, sort by class, apply metering where needed, and prepare everything for same-day USPS processing.

3

Same-Day Processing

Your mail is transported and submitted to USPS the same business day. No backlogs, no delays sitting in an office basket — your correspondence moves the day you need it to.

4

Consistent, Predictable Routine

Once your route is established, the schedule becomes something your team can depend on. No more chasing down who's handling the mail — it's handled, every day, without reminders.

Service Schedule

Our Houston postal service routes run Monday through Friday. Pickup windows are assigned based on your location and route, and we work with you to find a time that doesn't disrupt your business day.

Pricing

Custom Pricing for Your Mail Volume

No published rate cards — because no two businesses have the same mail operation. We quote based on your actual needs, not a one-size-fits-all package.

Volume-Based Pricing

Your rate is based on the volume and frequency of your outgoing mail. Higher-volume accounts benefit from lower per-piece pricing. We'll assess your average daily output before quoting.

Monthly Service Agreements

Most clients operate on a flat monthly agreement — predictable costs, no surprises. This covers your route, pickup labor, and standard mail processing. Postage costs are passed through at face value.
