How It Works

A Verified Chain of Custody From Your Office to Your Bank

Every deposit handled by Word's Mail Service follows a strict, documented process from pickup to confirmed bank delivery.

1

Scheduled Pickup at Your Office

At your designated pickup time, our trained courier arrives at your location to collect your bank deposit bag. Pickup time is consistent — scheduled around your daily close-of-business or deposit preparation routine.

2

Signed Chain of Custody

Your deposit is signed for at the point of pickup. This creates a documented record: who collected it, when, and from where. The deposit remains under our direct control from that moment through delivery.

3

Direct Delivery to Your Bank

Our courier delivers your deposit directly to your designated bank branch — not to a drop-off box, not to a third party. We deliver to the bank's receiving staff and obtain confirmation of receipt at delivery.

4

Confirmed Receipt & Documentation

Delivery confirmation is obtained at the bank. You have a complete paper trail: signed pickup, direct delivery, confirmed receipt — a clear and accountable record of every single deposit we handle for your business.

Security

Why Secure Bank Deposit Courier Matters

When an employee carries a bank deposit, they become a visible target. Cash, checks, and deposit bags transported by staff create exposure — for the employee and for the business. A single robbery or loss event can cost far more than months of professional courier service.

Professional bank deposit courier service removes that exposure. Your staff stays in the office. Your funds move through a trained, insured courier with a documented chain of custody that holds up under any audit or dispute.
